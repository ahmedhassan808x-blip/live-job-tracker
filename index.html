How the live data works

Your backend calls the JSearch API every 6 hours and caches recent finance openings across the 4 countries. This page reads that cached data. Because the backend (not your browser) talks to the job API, there are no CORS or scraping limits — this is the real automation the standalone file couldn't do.

Free-tier note: Render sleeps the service after ~15 min idle, so the first load after a while may take 30–60s to wake. Then it's fast.
